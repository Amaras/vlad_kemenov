# Chapter 62: Showing Feelings

Needless to say, the announcement that Katya was violently attacked was a shock for the whole Alliance.

Nobody knew, however, that the witch had died during the night.
The Kemenovs' curse was one of the clan's closely guarded secrets, because it efficiently protected their leader…
But Katya's state was critical enough to worry them all.

The different members of the secret group were both shocked an worried.
The attack had happened less than an hour after the end of the meeting between the Five Families and the Queen, but the students did not know any more.
Neither where she had been attacked, nor by who.
However, the attacker must have been very powerful or outnumbered her greatly to harm a former Auror and Dark witch.

Kevin, in Defence Against the Dark Arts class with Hufflepuffs, was relentlessly fighting with his professor.
Alastor Moody was not an idiot.
He had felt that the young Ravenclaw was anxious and that his magic was fluctuating wildly in response.
Because he was not a partisan of theoretical lectures and the usual practical works were not demandng enough to channel the young Wyllt's magic, the Professor had proposed training duels.
THen, without waiting for the pairs to be formed, he had attacked.
