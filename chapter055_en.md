# Chapter 55: Ball Presentations

A white veil covered the old manor of the Bones family.
December was coming to a close, and although it was rare, snow had fallen in London.

The Noble and Princely Bones Family had always lived in the middle of London.
Contrary to many old Pure-Blood families, they had never lived in the wizarding quarter, but in Westminster, the same neighbourhood as Buckingham Palace.
Few wizards and even fewer Muggles knew that five wizarding palaces were hidden along the impressive avenue linking Buckingham to Charring Cross.
Only the Bones family had kept it as their official home.

Amelia Bones observed the snowflakes fall on the large avenue from one of the patio door.
